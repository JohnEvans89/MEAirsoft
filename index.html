Welcome

Marines Elite provides opportunity for affordable, safe force-on-force training. We are proudly sponsored by and BioShot BB. If you want to practice real, force-on-force tactical scenarios, Marines Elite airsoft team can help provide that experience.

We enjoy the sport of Airsoft through military simulation. Our base of operation is located in Deptford, NJ and are proudly sponsored by BioShot BB. We focus our playing style on a milsim mind-set, and are always on the look out for like minded players. As our Team continues to grow, were are dedicated to having fun and growing the sport of airsoft. We have monthly games as well as weekly training sessions.
